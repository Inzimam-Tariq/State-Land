Lot Bandi System Guide
1. Select Location

Choose Tehsil and Mauza from the top filters to load land parcels.

2. Select Parcels

Click parcels directly on the map or from the compact list to add them into a Lot.

3. Review Selection

Selected khasras will appear in the right panel along with total Lot Area.

4. Assess & Save

Select zone information and click Save to finalize the Lot Bandi assessment.
